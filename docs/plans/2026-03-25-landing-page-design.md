# Landing Page Visual Refresh Design

**Date:** 2026-03-25

**Goal:** Elevate `landing.html` into a premium dark-tech AI SaaS landing page with stronger conversion focus, refined CTA hierarchy, and richer visual depth.

## Chosen Direction

- **Style:** Dark tech premium
- **Mood:** Secure, intelligent, high-conviction, conversion-oriented
- **Visual language:** Graphite backgrounds, amber-orange highlights, soft gold accents, glassmorphism cards, atmospheric glows

## Design Decisions

### Hero

- Keep the existing headline structure but make the section feel more premium through layered gradients, glow effects, and tighter text rhythm.
- Strengthen primary CTA emphasis and soften the secondary CTA into a glass-style button.
- Add trust/feature pills below the CTA row to create scanning anchors and product credibility.

### CTA Strategy

- Primary CTA should visually dominate through gradient fill, warm shadow, and hover elevation.
- Secondary CTA should remain visible but lower emphasis using translucent dark surfaces and subtle borders.
- CTA copy should feel more action-oriented and productized.

### Card Hierarchy

- Upgrade cards from flat dark panels to translucent glass panels with stronger inner borders and ambient shadows.
- Add a subtle top highlight strip and stronger icon badge treatment.
- Increase information rhythm using label, title, and short explanation hierarchy.

### Section Treatment

- Introduce faint grid texture and layered radial glows in hero and footer CTA.
- Use more section separation via background shifts and spacing contrast.
- Make the “Why Choose Us” section feel more like a premium value summary instead of a plain bullet list.

### Motion & Polish

- Use restrained hover movement: slight lift, border brightening, stronger shadow.
- Keep transition timing in the 200–300ms range.
- Maintain readability and accessibility with high text contrast and controlled highlight usage.

## Palette

- **Base:** `#020617`, `#0f172a`, `#111827`
- **Primary highlight:** `#f97316`
- **Secondary highlight:** `#fb923c`
- **Accent glow:** `#fbbf24`
- **Borders:** translucent white and orange overlays

## Typography

- Existing `Inter` + `Noto Sans SC` stack remains suitable.
- Tighten hero hierarchy with stronger max widths and improved tracking.
- Reduce paragraph brightness slightly for a more premium tone.

## Success Criteria

- Hero feels more premium and conversion-focused at first glance.
- CTA hierarchy is unmistakable.
- Cards feel layered and tactile rather than flat.
- Page keeps the original product messaging while looking more polished and expensive.
